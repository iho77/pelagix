About

Research and engineering, under one roof

Pelagix is a London-based technology company working at the intersection of artificial intelligence research and enterprise software engineering. We build intelligent systems that solve real operational problems — and keep running once they reach production.

Our work spans the full arc: early research and proof-of-concept, through deployment and long-term operation. The focus is AI agent architectures, language-model integration, and automation that connects modern AI to the systems a business already depends on.

We are a small, senior team — deliberately. It keeps the work close to the people doing it, and the standards high.

Services

What we do

Applied AI Research

Design and validation of language-model reasoning systems, multi-agent orchestration, and AI decision architectures shaped around real enterprise requirements — research turned into software that runs reliably at scale.

Enterprise Software Development

End-to-end delivery of production-grade systems — cloud-native platforms, API services, data pipelines, and domain-specific applications. Built for reliability, security by design, and long-term operability.

Intelligent Process Automation

Automation for complex, high-context workflows — connecting AI agents to enterprise data and business systems to reduce manual effort and improve consistency where it matters most.

Architecture & Consultancy

Guidance on AI system architecture, technology selection, and integration — from proof-of-concept through to a production roadmap, capability assessment, and technical due diligence.
