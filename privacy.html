1. Data controller

Pelagix Ltd ("Pelagix", "we", "us") is a private limited company incorporated in England and Wales, company number 17253464, registered office 128 City Road, London, EC1V 2NX. We are the data controller for personal data processed in connection with this website.

2. What we collect

This website uses no tracking cookies, analytics, advertising technology, or session recording, and carries no contact form.

If you contact us by email, we retain what you send — your name, email address, and the content of your message — in order to respond and to keep a record of business correspondence.

3. Legal basis

We process email correspondence under the legal basis of legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f) UK GDPR): our interest in responding to enquiries and maintaining business records. We do not process special categories of personal data.

4. Retention

Business correspondence is retained for a minimum of six years, consistent with standard UK record-keeping practice. You may request deletion of your correspondence at any time; we will comply unless a legal obligation requires us to retain the records.

5. Third-party services

This site loads typefaces from the Google Fonts CDN (fonts.googleapis.com, fonts.gstatic.com), which causes your browser to request files from Google's servers; Google's privacy policy applies to that request. No other third-party services, analytics, or tracking scripts are present.

6. Cookies

This website sets no cookies of any kind — essential, functional, analytics, or advertising.

7. Your rights

Under UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018 you may access, rectify, erase, restrict processing of, or obtain a portable copy of your personal data. To exercise any right, email hello@pelagix.co.uk. You may also complain to the Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk.

8. Changes

Material changes will be reflected in the date above. Continued use of this website after a change constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
